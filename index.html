Mission

The environmental benefits of solar energy have been preached since inception, however the economic barrier continues to deter this transition to renewables. Our mission is provide clients with an action plan that clarifies why they are an ideal client and the projected impacts of the transition and then facilitate the interaction with investment seekers who would mitigate the upfront costs and ultimately lead our communities to a better environment.

As a consultant in any field, the ultimate goal is to persuade and drive a client to pursue and positive change. Almost any clients focus is placed directly on economic gain, and although we hate to say it, money always comes first. No matter how bad climate change is getting, economic payback is the only incentive that will truly push a corporation to grow. Our purpose is to gather and analyze data for corporations, such as their electricity usage, operating hours and GHG emissions to be able to asses their candidacy for switching to renewable energy.

By providing a feasibility study, the corporation will understand the logistics, costs and risks surrounding implementation of the renewable energy system. Most importantly they will have a clear view of their economic savings in the long-run, as well as their positive environmental impact.

Logistics

Data for critical factors would be gathered and pulled into one database to enable simple analysis and characterization of a company. Here’s a sample of a few parameters that would be assessed:

  • 1. Electricity usage (kWh)
  • 2. Operating time (hours/week)
  • 3. Greenhouse gas emissions (kg)
  • 4. Size of infrastructure (m2)

Risk Assessment

Looking at the factors listed previously, a risk assessment would be developed looking at the following parameters that impact the feasibility of the project;

  • 1. Immediate & maintenance costs
  • 2. Implementation time
  • 3. Interest rates

Financial Planning

Our partnership breaks down any financial barrier that causes corporations to hesitate switching over to solar energy. Companies with money such as banks and private equity firms have the means to fund these renewable energy initiatives, but they have nothing driving them to do it. On the other hand, businesses and large corporations have the incentive to pursue renewable energy, but cannot afford the upfront costs. By merging these two stakeholders together, we act as a platform to facilitate implementation and lead our communities to a better environment.

Impact

Our approach to sustainability results in the following for our clients:

  • 1. Long-term economic savings
  • 2. Positive environmental impact
  • 3. Increased customer attraction

Sustainability

A sustainable future should be every company's goal, however the costs associated tend to deter company’s from pursuing renewable energy initiatives. If our service is accessible to these company’s their hesitation will be minimized as our feasibility studies ensure they have all the necessary information to implement a plan.

Soon, renewable energy will be the only way our earth survives. Companies who begin their transition now will act as leaders in environmental change and inspire others to follow their footsteps. Not only does this make their image stronger and brighter, but they are now an incentive for others because the change also attracts consumers.
